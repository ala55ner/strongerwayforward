# Stronger Way Forward — Landing Page

## Project Overview

A landing page for **Stronger Way Forward**, a motivational leadership brand with tagline **"Lead · Act · Move Forward"**. The site features Jamie Lassner as the primary subject — speaker, marathon runner, and leader.

## Brand Identity

- **Name:** Stronger Way Forward
- **Tagline:** Lead · Act · Move Forward
- **Visual Identity:** Navy blue + gold color palette; road-and-arrow logo symbolizing forward momentum
- **Tone:** Inspiring, action-oriented, personal
